# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive and technically honest community.
Constructive disagreement is welcome; harassment, threats, discrimination,
personal attacks and deliberate disruption are not.

Contributors should:

- focus feedback on code, behavior and evidence;
- respect different experience levels and backgrounds;
- disclose conflicts of interest;
- protect users by handling security reports privately;
- accept responsibility for mistakes and help repair them.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to contact@nito.network. Maintainers may edit
or remove content, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when necessary to protect the community.

This policy applies in project repositories, issue trackers, review channels and
official community spaces.
